## The MP4Fish Consortium

MP4Fish is a collaborative effort involving 8 partner institutions from 6 European countries.

---

### Coordinating Institution

**Institute of Marine Research (IMR)**
Bergen, Norway

The IMR leads the project coordination and is responsible for WP2 (Population Genomics) and WP5 (Dissemination). IMR hosts one of Europe's largest repositories of historical fish stock data, providing a unique foundation for integrating genomic data with long-term ecological records.

### Partner Institutions

**Alfred Wegener Institute (AWI)**
Bremerhaven, Germany

Leading WP1 (Molecular Stress Physiology). AWI brings expertise in polar and deep-sea marine biology, and operates state-of-the-art molecular laboratories for transcriptomic and proteomic analyses.

Brest, France

Contributing to WP3 (Local Adaptation) and WP4 (Management Tools). IFREMER provides expertise in fisheries stock assessment modelling and maintains key long-term monitoring datasets in the Bay of Biscay.

Vigo, Spain

## Advisory Board

MP4Fish is guided by an independent Scientific Advisory Board composed of five international experts in marine genomics, fisheries science, and conservation policy. The board meets annually to review project progress and provide strategic guidance.
