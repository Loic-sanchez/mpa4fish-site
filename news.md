### 📄 First genomic atlas of Atlantic cod published
*March 2026*

The MP4Fish consortium has published a high-resolution population genomic atlas of Atlantic cod (*Gadus morhua*) across its full North Atlantic distribution. The study, published in *Molecular Ecology*, identifies 14 genetically distinct population clusters and reveals contrasting patterns of adaptation to local thermal conditions. The dataset is freely available on NCBI (BioProject PRJNAXXXXXX).

### 🌡️ Thermal stress transcriptomics in European anchovy
*January 2026*

Results from WP1 reveal a conserved suite of heat-shock and oxidative stress genes activated in European anchovy under thermal stress, with significant differences in response magnitude between Atlantic and Mediterranean populations. A preprint is available on bioRxiv.

[Read the preprint →](https://biorxiv.org/) *(placeholder link)*

### 🎓 MP4Fish Summer School 2025 — Report
*September 2025*

The first MP4Fish Summer School was held in Naples, Italy, hosted by the Stazione Zoologica Anton Dohrn. 24 early-career researchers from 11 countries participated in a week-long programme covering population genomics, bioinformatics, and science communication. All course materials are openly available on our [Zenodo community](https://zenodo.org/).

---

### 🚀 Project Kick-off Meeting
*February 2024*

The MP4Fish consortium held its kick-off meeting in Bergen, Norway. All eight partner institutions were represented. The meeting established the governance structure, sampling protocols, and data management plan for the first project year.
